Frequently Asked Questions - Know more about our LMS
What is a Learning Management System (LMS)?

A Learning Management System (LMS) is a software application for administering, documenting, tracking, reporting, and delivering educational courses or training programs.

 How do I create an account?

To create an account, visit our registration page, fill in the required details, and follow the prompts to set up your profile.

 Can I access courses on my mobile device?

Yes, our LMS is designed to be mobile-friendly, allowing you to access courses and materials from your smartphone or tablet.

 What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on the 'Forgot Password?' link on the login page, and follow the instructions to reset it.

 How can I contact support?

You can contact our support team via the 'Contact Us' section on our website or by sending an email to support@example.com.
